Domain 2 – Question 2.2 (Effect of Adhering to Intervention)
-----------------------------------------------------------

Prompt:
You are evaluating Risk of Bias Domain 2 for the “Effect of Adhering to Intervention.”
This is Question 2.2:
“Were carers and people delivering the interventions aware of participants' assigned intervention during the trial?”

Read the provided document.

Your tasks:

1. Decide whether carers/people delivering the intervention knew (or likely knew) participant allocations.
2. Treat them as aware if blinding was absent or ineffective, side effects/toxicities signaled allocation, or allocation concealment was not maintained.
3. Treat them as unaware when masking procedures for deliverers were used and appear effective.
4. Return one of the following:
   Y  = Yes (carers/deliverers were aware)
   PY = Probably Yes
   PN = Probably No
   N  = No (carers/deliverers were not aware)
   NI = No Information
5. Provide a short justification explaining why the chosen answer is appropriate.
6. Quote exact sentences or phrases from the document that support your conclusion.
7. If no information about awareness of carers/deliverers is provided, answer NI.

Output format:

Answer: <Y | PY | PN | N | NI>
Justification: <2–4 sentences explaining reasoning>
Quoted Evidence:
