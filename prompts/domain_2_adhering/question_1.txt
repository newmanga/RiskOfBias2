Domain 2 – Question 2.1 (Effect of Adhering to Intervention)
-----------------------------------------------------------

Prompt:
You are evaluating Risk of Bias Domain 2 for the “Effect of Adhering to Intervention.”
This is Question 2.1:
“Were participants aware of their assigned intervention during the trial?”

Read the provided document.

Your tasks:

1. Determine whether participants knew (or likely knew) which intervention they were receiving during the trial.
2. Treat participants as aware if the study reports unblinded participants, mentions side effects or toxicities unique to one arm that participants could identify, or otherwise indicates participants could tell what they were allocated.
3. Consider participants unaware when blinding/placebo/sham methods were used and appear effective.
4. Return one of the following:
   Y  = Yes (participants were aware)
   PY = Probably Yes
   PN = Probably No
   N  = No (participants were not aware)
   NI = No Information
5. Provide a short justification explaining why the chosen answer is appropriate.
6. Quote exact sentences or phrases from the document that support your conclusion.
7. If no information about participant awareness is provided, answer NI.

Output format:

Answer: <Y | PY | PN | N | NI>
Justification: <2–4 sentences explaining reasoning>
Quoted Evidence:
"<exact text copied from the document>"
